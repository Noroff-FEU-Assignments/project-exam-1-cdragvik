About me

My name is Frankie, and my biggest passion is FOOD! I am constantly looking for new recipes and ideas for good food experiences. I made this blog because i wanted a place where i can document my journey and collect all of my recipes.

In my everyday life i focus on simple and fairly healty dishes full of flavour. On the weekends I allow myself to be a litte more creative, and if I have the time I like to cook a three-course dinner for my family on Saturday night.

I have tried and failed hundreds of times over the years. My biggest tips is to just learn from your mistakes and keep going. Cooking is so much fun when you can experiment on your own!
